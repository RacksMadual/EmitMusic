Tyler the Creator reveals his favorite artists of 2022

From Steve Lacy to Kendrick Lamar, the Ladera Heights native shares his favorite's for the year 2022

By Prince without the M

Tyler, the Creator‘s favorite songs of the year are here. One week after JAY-Z made a TIDAL playlist of his favorite 2022 tracks, Tyler followed with a tweet of his top songs, with Steve Lacy, Pusha T, Kendrick Lamar and more making the list.

King Push, who received a Best Rap Album Grammy nomination for his fourth studio album It’s Almost Dry, topped the list with his Jay-Z and Pharrell Williams collaboration “Neck & Wrist.” Tyler’s 2022 collaboration with Williams, “Cash In Cash Out,” which also features 21 Savage, also made his list of best songs.

Tyler, the Creator also gave a shoutout to Odd Future affiliate Steve Lacy for the track “Buttons” from Lacy’s sophomore album Gemini Rights. In an August Billboard profile, Lacy credited Tyler for assisting him with the chorus for “Bad Habit,” making the song a Billboard Hot 100 top-charter and a TikTok hit.
