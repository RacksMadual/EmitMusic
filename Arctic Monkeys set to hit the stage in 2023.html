Arctic Monkeys set to hit the stage in 2023

The group kicked off their much-anticipated tour in Linz, Austria last night (24 April) and played some of their greatest hits.

By Prince without the M

The band, fronted by Alex Turner, will be performing to fans in Coventry, Manchester, Middlesbrough, Norwich, Sheffield, Swansea, Southampton, London, Dublin and Glasgow. They will also be headlining at Glastonbury alongside Elton John, Gun N’ Roses and Lizzo.The Arctic Monkeys have been nominated for the ‘Best Album’ category at this year’s Ivor Novello Awards. The Sheffield-formed rock band’s newest album, entitled ‘The Car’, has made the shortlist of just five albums for the award. Other artists nominated for the category include Fontaines D.C, Little Simz, SAULT, and Obongjayar. The awards will be held in London on 18 May 23 at Grosvenor House in London and fans are excited to celebrate exceptional songwriting and composition in music from the UK and Ireland.
