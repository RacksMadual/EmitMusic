Skrillex and Flowdan link up with Fred again for his first piece of music of 2023

Skrillex shares “Rumble” featuring Fred again.. and Flowdan

By Jordan Darville

The new song is a throwback to dubstep’s origins, way before Skrillex helped popularize a more candy-crushed version of the subgenre. In a nod to the sound Flowdan helped nurture, “Rumble” is a stripped-down battle cry, its sub-bass yawning like a hungry predator amidst flickering machine gun kicks and Flowdan’s hungry verses.

Last year, Fred again.. previewed a new Skrillex collaboration at gigs that included a hugely popular Boiler Room set. That track, which features grime and dubstep elder Flowdan, became hotly sought after in a rare way and hinted at a possible underground renaissance for Skrillex after the chart-conquering J. Balvin collaboration “In Da Getto.”
