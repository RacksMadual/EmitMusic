Yves Tumor starts his European tour in Spain

The new album, Praise a Lord Who Chews but Which Does Not Consume; (Or Simply, Hot Between Worlds), arrives in March via Warp

By Prince without the M

Yves Tumor has announced a new album: Praise a Lord Who Chews but Which Does Not Consume; (Or Simply, Hot Between Worlds) is due out March 17 via Warp. The follow-up to 2020’s Heaven to a Tortured Mind features production from Noah Goldstein and was mixed by Alan Moulder. Accompanying the announcement, Tumor has unveiled a new song called “Echolalia,” which arrives with a music video directed by frequent collaborator Jordan Hemingway. Yves Tumor is heading out on tour later this year. After performing during both weekends of Coachella, Tumor will head east, with dates across North America and Europe.
