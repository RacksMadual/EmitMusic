Jamiroquai will be headlining the Metronome festival in June

The festival will be held for the sixth time at the Prague Exhibition Center.

By Prince without the M

During their twenty-six-year journey through the funk galaxy, Jamiroquai achieved many notable successes. Since emerging from London's acid jazz underground, Jamiroquai have released eight studio albums, which have sold over 26 million copies worldwide. Jamiroquai have reached the UK Top 10 seven times (three #1 albums), won a Grammy, been nominated for a Brit Award and are also listed in the Guinness Book of World Records as the best-selling funk album of all time. The name Jamiroquai refers to the Iroquois Indian tribe and suggests an understanding of their tragic fate.
